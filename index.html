About me

My dream as a child was to be a scientist, astronaut, and archaeologist... Yes, it was an AND not a OR. My mom and grandma taught me to always aim high and search endless for knowledge. Go big or go home, right?! Over the years, my hunger for knowledge has not diminished which led me to an academic career.
